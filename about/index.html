About me

Hi, welcome to my website!

I'm currently busy completing my Masters degree in Methodolgy and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences. I have a background in Psychology and a special interest in (Bio)medical and health-related research.

My two passions are statistics and graphic design, so you could say that I'm “statistician by day, illustrator by night"! I wholeheartedly believe that knowledge is as valuable as you can communicate it. I help researchers to make their ideas more accessible, understandable and fun.

When you don't find me working on my laptop or drawing on the iPad, I'll probably be in the gym. My latest obsession is my fitbit, which I highly recommend if you are an active statistics nerd hungry for data and plots! It helps to comprise your current wellbeing into sleep scores, resting heart rates and other fun stuff, it's great!

On this website I focus on sharing my projects regarding statistics and research. A second website regarding scientific illustrations and creative work is comming soon!
